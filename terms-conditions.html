CARPOOL VOTE ORGANIZATION

Carpool Vote is a volunteer-run open-source project that connects volunteer drivers with anybody who needs a ride in order to vote.

Volunteer drivers have no employment relationship with Carpool Vote.

Carpool Vote provides technology services supporting one way for drivers and riders to find each other and arrange a ride during the election.
