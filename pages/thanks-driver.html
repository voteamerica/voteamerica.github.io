What happens next?

Our system will match you with a passenger(s), based on the information you've provided. We'll send you a notification of every ride request that matches your criteria. You can then accept as many ride requests as work for you.

Once you have accepted a ride request, we'll send you a message with the rider's contact details. It's then up the you and the rider to arrange when and where you'd like to meet, and any other details of your trip.
